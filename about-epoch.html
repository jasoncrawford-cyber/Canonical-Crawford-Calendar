Chosen Epoch

SMLC Year 1, Eastren 1 is defined as:

Gregorian: 2000-03-20 (UTC date reference)
Astronomical anchor: March Equinox season-start reference

Rationale

  • Astronomical neutrality: equinox is physically defined and culture-independent.
  • Seasonal symmetry: month system centers equinoxes/solstices mid-month.
  • Modern reference: avoids uncertainty and reform artifacts of historical epochs.
  • Deterministic closure: 334-year / 4131-month cycle eliminates drift by construction.

What changing the epoch would do

The calendar rules remain identical, but every labeled date shifts. The system is internally exact; epoch selection is the naming origin.
