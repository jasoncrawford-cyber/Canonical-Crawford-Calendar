Epoch Definition & Justification

SMLC Year 1, Eastren 1
Gregorian reference: 2000-03-20
Astronomical anchor: March Equinox season-start reference

Rationale

  • Astronomical neutrality: equinox is physically defined and culture-independent.
  • Seasonal symmetry: equinoxes/solstices centered mid-month.
  • Modern reference: avoids uncertainty of ancient observations.
  • Deterministic closure: 334-year / 4131-month cycle prevents drift by construction.
